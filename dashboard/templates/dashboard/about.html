The Story

XPENSEDIARY began in a cramped Mumbai apartment when our founders realized they had five different financial apps, yet still didn't know where their money went at the end of the month.

The problem wasn't a lack of tools; it was a lack of presence. Most apps required you to leave your life to record your life.

We realized that tracking expenses shouldn't feel like a chore. It should happen where you already spend your time. In 2022, we built the first prototype that lived inside WhatsApp, and the response was overwhelming.
